About the Show

Funopolis is a brand new American animated television series created by Ayan Prasad. The series premiered on February 26, 2023 and still in the top ten TV shows in the US. This show is made for RAMS Junior channel in US.

This show revolves around the three main characters of the show; Aryan,Becky and Connor a.k.a the 'ABC' trio of Funopolis. Come and experience the excitement as they explore the city of Funopolis through their curious eyes, embark on some of the biggest adventurous, learn all kinds of life skills while helping their community and have endless fun. The trio's every day challenges in this imaginary world, sets the stage for our young viewers as they watch our awesome trio put on their detective hat to solve mysteries, make amazing discoveries, save the environment and do so much more.......
