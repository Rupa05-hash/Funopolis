Meet Funopolis Pals

Aryan




Aryan - the 'Action' boy! Aryan is a happy 7 year old boy with a positive outlook in life. He is an 'action' boy who is always on the go. His favorite actvity is to create animations. Check out some of his latest animations here. Other than the virtual world of animations, Aryan's passions are basketball and piano. He loves to play travel basketball for his school team. He is captain-like and can jump as high as the moon. Aryan cares for his friends very much. He would do anything to cheer his friends. help them to find the happy side in everything. Basically he is a jolly good fellow who is loved by friends, family and people around him.

Becky




A 8-year-old sweet little girl who is loved by everyone. She stands for her friendship with Aryan and Connner. She likes to be in every place around Aryan and Connner. Becky has a thing for adventures. She imagines herself to be a 'Wonder girl' and loves to put on different costumes and hairstyles to match her imaginative and adventurous mind. She always looks for an opportunity to go on an expedition. Usually through Becky's expeditions, the trio encounters the problem that needs to be solved.

Connor




Connor is a smart and curious 7 year old kid who likes to explore the world around him and ask questions. He puts on his 'curious' hat all the time and solve riddles for hours. People like to call him 'Curious Connor'. Like Becky and Aryan, Connor has a passion for music too. He plays Guitar for his school band. Connor is funny and helpful.He would do anything for his best friends - Aryan and Becky. Connor loves nature. He wants people to 'Go Green' and save the environment.
